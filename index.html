How Ethical are Mindfulness apps?

Mediation apps available on the app store market themselves as the one-stop solution for stressed-out individuals who cannot seem to catch a break. The golden solution to society’s modern problems is these apps, right? The creators behind these apps must be angels! Well… In this blog post, we examine how these Mediation apps mass-collect your personal information and usage data and how they could even be sold to third parties.

Time Is Money: Food Ordering Apps

Many people utilize food ordering apps for their convenience and discounts, but given their possible effect on consumers spending habits, are they helping or harming their amount spent annually? In this podcast my team of college students discuss topics related to food ordering apps and offer our advice on how to save money when using them.
